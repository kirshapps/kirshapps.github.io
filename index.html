Fun

Pokaface is incredibly fun. Once you start playing it you, you can't stop. With Pokaface, you get this rush of energy when you play urging you to keep playing, and beat your highscore.

Leaderboards and Acheivments

Pokaface includes a whole lot of features to help you interact with your friends, without even leaving the app. Pokaface includes Leaderboards, Acheivments and even a store to buy stuff to help you out.

Online Capabilities

Once you've beat that highscore, you want to share it with the world. Using Pokaface, you can share your highscores to Facebook, Twitter and more.
