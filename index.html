Introducing Tap-a-dot
Tap the dots to earn points

When you download Tap-a-dot, you will realize how much fun it really is.

Fun

Tap-a-dot is incredibly fun. Once you start playing it you, you can't stop. With tap-a-dot, you get this rush of energy when you play urging you to keep playing, and beat your highscore.

Leaderboards and Acheivments

Tap-a-dot includes a whole lot of features to help you interact with your friends, without even leaving the app. Tap-a-dot includes Leaderboards, Acheivments and even a store to buy stuff to help you out.

Online Capabilities

Once you've beat that highscore, you want to share it with the world. Using Tap-a-dot, you can share your highscores to Facebook, Twitter and more.
